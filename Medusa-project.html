Medusa

Product Overview

I am currently designing Medusa, a social media app that blends casual sharing with AI-powered discovery to inspire curiosity about the world around us. Combining the spontaneity of BeReal with the visual inspiration of Pinterest, Medusa encourages users to post about their everyday environments—whether it’s a street mural, an architectural landmark, or a unique object. Using Google Image Search and Gemini’s visual ML capabilities, Medusa identifies elements in each post, providing educational insights and "discover similar" recommendations to turn everyday encounters into moments of discovery.

The Approach

I am currently designing Medusa’s interface in Figma, focusing on an intuitive posting flow that minimizes friction. When users upload a photo, Google Image Search and Gemini process the image to recognize artworks, historical landmarks, or cultural elements. The app then enhances the post with relevant educational details and visually similar discoveries, allowing users to explore related content. The feed is designed to be interactive, enabling engagement through reactions, comments, and shared discoveries.

Current Progress & Next Steps

I am developing detailed wireframes and high-fidelity UI assets. The design process is focused on refining the posting experience, ensuring that AI-powered insights feel natural and engaging. Medusa is shaping into a platform that redefines social posting by encouraging curiosity, learning, and exploration in everyday life.
